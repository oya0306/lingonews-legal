LingoNews — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

1. Overview

LingoNews ("we", "our", "the app") is an English learning application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, which third-party services help operate the app, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: When you create an account or sign in with email/password, Sign in with Apple, or Google, we may receive your email address, display name, user identifier, and authentication provider information.
  • Learning data: Quiz scores, saved vocabulary, selected teacher, language settings, and learning progress may be stored to provide feedback and track your progress.
  • Conversation practice data: During conversation practice, we may process your voice audio, speech transcript, conversation history, selected article or topic context, and AI feedback results.
  • Voice recordings: Voice recordings are used for speech recognition and conversation practice. They are processed to generate transcripts and are not intended to be permanently stored by LingoNews.
  • Subscription data: Subscription status, purchase identifiers, and entitlement information may be processed through Apple, Google Play, and RevenueCat. We do not store credit card numbers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and improve the app
  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To provide article reading, vocabulary quizzes, and conversation practice
  • To generate speech transcripts, AI conversation responses, and learning feedback
  • To save vocabulary, scores, settings, and progress
  • To manage subscriptions and premium access
  • To support account deletion and privacy requests

4. Third-Party AI Services and Data Sharing

Conversation practice may send your voice audio, speech transcript, conversation history, selected article or topic context, and related learning feedback data to third-party AI and speech services.

These services may include Deepgram, OpenAI, and Google Gemini. They are used for speech recognition, text-to-speech, AI conversation responses, and learning feedback generation.

We do not use this data for advertising or tracking. The app asks for your permission before sending personal conversation practice data to third-party AI services.

5. Other Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, storage, and backend functions
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and Apple in-app purchases
  • Google — Google sign-in, Google Play billing, and Google Gemini AI services
  • Deepgram — speech-to-text and text-to-speech processing
  • OpenAI — conversation feedback and language analysis
  • RevenueCat — subscription entitlement management

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the app. Some temporary audio or processing data may be retained only as needed to complete the requested feature.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the app by going to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deleting your account removes your LingoNews account and associated app data from our systems, subject to legal, security, and operational retention requirements.

Deleting your LingoNews account does not automatically cancel an active Apple or Google Play subscription. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple Account or Google Play account.

8. Children's Privacy

LingoNews is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by using the in-app account deletion option or by contacting us at the email below.
