Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in?

LingoNews uses Google Sign-In. Tap "Sign in with Google" on the login screen and follow the prompts.

How do I change my difficulty level?

On the home screen, tap the difficulty tabs (初級 / 中級 / 上級) to switch between beginner, intermediate, and advanced articles.

How does the free trial work?

New users get a 3-day free trial with full access. After the trial, a subscription is required to continue using conversation practice and other premium features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → LingoNews.

The microphone isn't working

Make sure you've granted LingoNews microphone permission. Go to Settings → LingoNews → Microphone and enable it.
